About Me

Eivind Norling


My name is Eivind Norling aka Ewy or EwyBoy. I'm 25 years old, and I have been developing Minecraft mods for way over 10 years now. Over the years my mods have in total accumulated over 60,000,000 downloads across 35 different projects on CurseForge. When not making mods for Minecraft I am studying applied computer science at University of Bergen.

The things I do

I am a one-man band when it comes to creating mods and for the most part I work alone. I have chosen to learn a bit of everything rather than specializing in one field.

Here is the things I do


I make my own 3D models from scratch in BlockBench.

I create all my textures, logos and pixel art from scratch using PhotoShop.

I learned sound remastering and editing while running my own podcast.

I have created my own libraries to automate anything you need to create a Minecraft mod.

All my development is streamed live to Twitch so people can tag along and perhaps learn something.

All my mods, assets and resources are open-source and distributed under the MIT-License.
